The Best According to Angela

My top 3 favorite movies of all time.

1.The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

This film follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly imprisoned for murder, who befriends fellow inmate Red. In the harsh environment of Shawshank prison, Andy finds hope and redemption. It consistently ranks at the top of IMDb’s Top 250 Movies and is a must-watch for its uplifting and timeless themes.

2.The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this mafia classic chronicles the Corleone family’s struggles for power and survival in post-WWII America. With memorable performances and a gripping narrative, it set the standard for crime dramas and won multiple Academy Awards, including Best Picture.

3.The Dark Knight (2008)

This superhero film, directed by Christopher Nolan, portrays the intense battle between Batman and his nemesis, the Joker. Heath Ledger’s iconic performance as the Joker and the film’s gripping story made it a landmark in the superhero genre, often cited as one of the greatest films ever made.
